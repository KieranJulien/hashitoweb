Hashito's opinions

I: Respecting Opinions

An opinion is a view or judgment formed about something, not necessarily based on fact or knowledge. Love them or hate
them, we all have them, and everyone's opinion is all different from each other. Opinions may offend you, but we need to
respect them. Speak about opinions respectfully, and listen to other people's opinions respectfully. That is the only way you
will ever gain any sort of power, or influence. Your voice is powerful.

II: Wrong or Right

How do you tell a right opinion from a wrong opinion? There are no universally wrong or right opinions, because they are,
well, just opinions. It is very well up to you whether an opinion is wrong, or right. For example, if somebody says Hypixel is a
bad server, I would say their opinion is wrong. However, to others, their opinion may be right. That is how it works, and we
need to accept everyone's opinions if we are ever going to get somewhere.

III: Opinions fuel Hypixel

This is a fact. Whenever we make a suggestion, an idea, a guide, we use OUR voices, OUR knowledge, OUR opinion. This
community would never be formed if opinions didn't exist. HOW did we get these minigames? HOW did we get all this amazing
ideas. OPINIONS, ideas from parties both agreeing and disagreeing bouncing back and forth between one another that is
EXACTLY how great ideas are formed.
